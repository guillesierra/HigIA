# Legal And Ethical Notes

HigIA must remain a public-data, non-identifiable-data project.

## Rules

- Do not scrape personal data.
- Do not scrape or publish identifiable health data.
- Do not bypass access controls, login walls, CAPTCHAs, robots restrictions, or private APIs.
- Do not publish raw datasets unless redistribution is allowed.
- Always store source URL, access date, known license/status, and acquisition notes.
- Keep public JSON exports limited to aggregated, publishable data.

## Asturias And SESPA

Internal SESPA datasets are outside the public MVP. If access is obtained later, each dataset must be classified by permission, sensitivity, aggregation level, and publication constraints before ingestion or export.
